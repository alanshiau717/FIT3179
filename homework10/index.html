Crime in San Fransico

This visualisation explores the different dimensions of crime data in order to reveal interesting trends in the data. Looking at the Bar chart, it can be seen that for every single police district, the most common crime is Larceny/Theft. Looking at crimes by location, we can also reveal an interesting pattern whereby generally speaking, the further you are from the North-East section of San Fransico, the lower the crime per capita becomes.
